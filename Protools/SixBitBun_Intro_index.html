SixBit Bun™

Nibble your images down to pure code.

SixBit Bun™ is your friendly, sovereign batch image → base64 converter — created for illustrators, web designers, teachers, and developers who want clean embeds without ever uploading files to a server. Everything runs 100% in your browser on your own device.

What SixBit Bun can do:
  • Convert many images at once (PNG, JPG, GIF, WebP, SVG) into ready-to-embed base64 code.
  • Optionally resize images by max width and adjust JPEG quality for lighter pages.
  • Strip the data: prefix when you only need the raw base64 string.
  • Minify SVG (remove comments/extra spaces) for compact, clean vector code.
  • Choose whether to wrap lines for easier reading and copy/paste.
  • Use per-image buttons to Copy, Download (.b64.txt), or create a tiny BunLink (.sbbun.json) file.
  • Keep a simple History of your recent conversions (this browser session only).
How to use this page:
  • 1. Scroll down to the SixBit Bun app, then drag in images or click the drop area to choose files.
  • 2. Set optional resize width / JPEG quality, and toggle strip prefix, minify SVG, or wrap lines.
  • 3. Click Convert All. A card appears for each image with its preview and base64 code.
  • 4. Use Copy to put base64 on your clipboard, Download to save a text file, or BunLink to create a small JSON file that you can bundle into KhaeZip™ Sovereign archives or other Pearls Gamut tools.
  • 5. Nothing is ever uploaded or stored on our servers. Your images and code stay with you.

SixBit Bun™ is part of the My Art Gallery & Khaemenes Academy tool family and is designed to play nicely with KhaeZip™, Prose™, ARSHIF, and the wider Pearls Gamut ecosystem.
