How to use SixBit Bun

  • 1. Drag in or click to choose images (PNG, JPG, GIF, WebP, SVG).
  • 2. (Optional) Set max width or JPEG quality. SVG: can minify.
  • 3. Click Convert All. Preview and base64 code appears instantly!
  • 4. Copy code (one or all), or download as .txt for easy use in code, HTML, or JSON.
  • 5. All work is local; nothing is saved or uploaded.
  • 6. History button shows your last 10 conversions this session.
  • 7. "Strip prefix" removes data:image/png;base64, etc from output.
  • 8. Minify SVG strips whitespace/comments from SVG code (SVG only).
  • 9. Wrap lines for easier copy-paste.
Tip: Nothing is uploaded. Everything stays on your device.
