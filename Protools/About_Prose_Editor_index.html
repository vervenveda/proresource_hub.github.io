🖋️ About the Prose™ Editing Suite

Prose™ is a sovereign writing sanctuary for authors, educators, students, and creators — a calm, intelligent workspace that lives entirely in your browser and honors your ownership from first draft to final export.

✨ Core Features

  • Full Editing Suite: Rich formatting (bold/italic/underline/strike), alignment, lists, indents, blockquotes, superscript/subscript, horizontal rules, links, tables, code blocks, and image inserts — plus drag-and-drop image uploads.
  • Beautiful Font + Size Control: Sacred fonts and a cleaner reading experience with quick A / A+ presets (Small/Normal/Large/Huge) and an Advanced size list when you need precise control.
  • Pro Layout Tools: Writing-friendly structure support including outlines / navigation, document-ready spacing tools, and polished formatting options designed for professional drafts.
  • Pro Tools Sidebar: One-click access to Find & Replace, Comments, Templates/Snippets, Table of Contents, Writing Goals, Footnotes, Thesaurus, Versions, Export, and Restore.
  • Aureon + AuroraCore AI Companion: A built-in assistant for rewrites, summaries, expansions, paraphrases, critiques, and analysis — with creative style modes and refinement tools for flow, rhythm, mood, cohesion, and vocabulary.
  • Insert Starter: A gentle “starter seed” button that inserts only when you choose — and never overwrites existing writing.
  • Focus + Theme Modes: Focus Mode for distraction-free writing, plus paper/night/gold/silver themes for comfort and readability.
  • Auto-Save + Versions: Local autosave with version snapshots and quick restore so you can safely experiment and time-travel through drafts.
  • Offline-Friendly & Local: No logins, no accounts, no tracking — your work stays on your device via local storage.
  • Multi-Format Export: Export to PDF, TXT, Markdown, HTML, DOC, or EPUB for printing, sharing, or archiving in your own system.
